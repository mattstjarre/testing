About KMS Consulting

Where Deep Expertise Meets Innovative Technology

KMS Consulting was founded by three industry veterans who share a common vision: to help organizations unlock their full potential through intelligent process improvement and thoughtful technology integration.

With combined experience spanning over six decades at Fortune 500 companies like GE, Pratt & Whitney, and Alstom, our team brings unparalleled expertise in enterprise transformation, IT strategy, and operational excellence.

We believe that the best solutions come from understanding both the technology and the people who use it. That's why we take a human-centered approach to AI implementation, ensuring that automation enhances rather than replaces human expertise.
